How to Reach Dandeli

Dandeli can be approached by bus, both state-run and private operators or can be reached in private transport or taxi as well. It is around 97 km from Goa while in Karnataka it can be approached from Hubballi and Belagavi which are two district towns located at 75 and 66 km respectively from Dandeli. From Bengaluru, Dandeli is situated at a distance of 459km and from Pune it is 440km. Tourists can also be transferred from airports located at Hubli, Belagavi, and Goa.

From Bangalore (Train, Bus, Flight):

Train: If you wish to travel from Bengaluru to Dandeli by train, there are no direct trains available since Dandeli has no railway station. However, one can take the Rani Chennamma train (16589) which goes up to Belagavi (66km from Dandeli) or till Londa junction (around 37km from Dandeli). From Londa or Belagavi, you can take a bus or taxi to Dandeli. Another option is to board the Janashatabdi Express (2079) and get down at Hubli Junction and from there take a bus to Dandeli or hire a cab. Hubli is 75km from Dandeli. Night trains are available and various destinations can be reached by morning.

Bus: Buses are also a convenient mode of transport. Private operators (SRS/VRL) ply daily between the two destinations. Dandeli is around 477km from Bangalore and it takes about 9hrs to reach. The route taken is AH 47/NH 4 via Bangalore, Haveri, Davanagere, Chitradurga, Hiriyur, Tumkur, and Dandeli.

Things to Do in Dandeli

Ziplining

For fans of wildlife tourism, Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary is a wonderful getaway to enjoy some time in the company of lush green forests and wild animals. Located on the Western Ghats, this sanctuary is acclaimed for having a range of diverse flora and fauna. It is amongst the few places to visit in Dandeli that boasts of an incredible landscape which is filled with river valleys, verdant hills and scenic beauty.

Camping

The most popular and the most thrilling things to do in Dandeli is river rafting on the River Kali which is a treat for adventure enthusiasts. This river rafting activity in Dandeli is conducted on the rapids of the Kali River ranging from grade I to IV. This thrilling activity will surely pump some adrenaline into your body and provide a thrilling experience as you raft over gushing waters amidst the scenic beauty of the forest.

Located in the Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary, Ulavi Caves is a beautiful place to visit in Dandeli that offers an amazing experience to explore ancient rock-cut caves. These caves are surrounded by lush greenery and are a perfect spot to enjoy a peaceful retreat. The caves are an ancient religious site and are believed to have been inhabited by monks and sages in the past.
